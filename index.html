Bio

Born and raised the daughter of a minister, Kyler grew up in Cincinnati with her family. She was a lead singing gospel vocalist and performed at many services and public ceremonies. During her college years, Kyler began her professional training for modeling and commercial work at NV Models. She later signed to the talent roster of the agency. It was not long after signing with NV Models that Kyler had her first taste of acting at age 21 when she was cast for a supporting role in “The Black Dove Film.” After earning a Bachelor of Arts in Communications from Xavier University Kyler relocated to New York City. After a five-year residency in NYC, Kyler relocated to Los Angeles, California where she currently pursues artful expressions from singing, writing and acting to dance, theatre and modeling.
